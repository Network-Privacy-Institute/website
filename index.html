Our Mission

Network Privacy Institute is an independent initiative dedicated to strengthening privacy, security, and freedom of expression online. We support public-interest infrastructure that helps keep the internet open, resilient, and accessible.

Current Project

Tor Relays

Our first objective is to deploy and maintain Tor relays that contribute bandwidth and resilience to the Tor network.

Transparency

We are committed to publishing operational updates, infrastructure information, and funding reports.

Community Support

We aim to build sustainable community funding to support privacy-preserving internet infrastructure.

Roadmap

  • Launch our first Tor relay
  • Expand to 3 relays
  • Publish transparency reports
  • Establish sustainable funding
  • Support additional privacy technologies
